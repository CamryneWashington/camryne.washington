Author, Debra A. K. Thompson needed a website that met accessibility demands. The author has a vision impairment and relys on screen readers and contrasted colors to read content. Through Wordpress CMS, I styled the site with custom HTML and Elementor. As part of the redesign process, I also saw the need to change the overall layout of the website to increase appeal, traffic flow, and product promotion. The website now captures visitors which is over 100 people a month. With careful selection of colors that are WCAG compliant, the site was transformed into a more visually appealing website for both customers and her.
